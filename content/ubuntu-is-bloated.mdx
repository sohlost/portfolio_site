# My Reading and Content Recommendations

Welcome to my curated list of reading and content recommendations! This page will serve as a living document that I'll update regularly with new discoveries and favorites. Whether you're looking for books, articles, podcasts, or other forms of content, I hope you find something valuable here.

## Books

### Fiction

* **"Project Hail Mary" by Andy Weir** - A gripping sci-fi novel that combines scientific accuracy with an engaging story about survival and friendship.
* **"The Midnight Library" by Matt Haig** - A thought-provoking exploration of life's infinite possibilities and the choices we make.

### Non-Fiction

* **"Atomic Habits" by James Clear** - A practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones, backed by scientific research.
* **"Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" by Yuval Noah Harari** - A fascinating journey through the history of our species, from ancient times to the present.

## Articles

* **"The Art of Learning"** - An insightful piece on how to effectively learn new skills and retain information.
* **"The Future of Work"** - A look at how technology is reshaping the workplace and what it means for the future.

## Podcasts

* **"Lex Fridman Podcast"** - Engaging conversations with leading thinkers and innovators across various fields.
* **"The Tim Ferriss Show"** - Interviews with world-class performers to extract tools and tactics you can use.

## Online Courses

* **"Machine Learning" by Andrew Ng** - A comprehensive introduction to machine learning, perfect for beginners.
* **"The Science of Well-Being" by Yale University** - A course that explores the science of happiness and how to apply it to your life.

## Stay Tuned

I'll be updating this list regularly with new recommendations. Feel free to check back often for fresh content!
